Welcome

Sharing is who I am, and teaching is where I am at my best. Teaching is my meaningful way to be a part of the creative community.

I'm a digital designer & certified Adobe instructor. I've also won multiple Adobe teaching awards at their annual MAX conference.

I grew up in New Zealand & now live in Ireland. I’ve spent a long time watching others learn, and teach, to refine how I work with you to be efficient, useful and, most importantly, memorable. I want you to carry what I’ve shown you into a bright future.

I have a wife (a lovely Irish girl) and kids. I have lived and worked in many places (as Kiwis tend to do) – but most of my 15+ years of creating and teaching has had one overriding theme: bringing others along for the ride as we all try to change the world with our stories, our labours of love and our art.

- Dan
